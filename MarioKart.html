Mario Kart 

What Is Mario Kart? 

Mario Kart is a racing game, there are 4 game modes in Mario Kart: Grand Prix, Time Trials, VS, and Battle Mode. Grand Prix involves a race through each of the four races in one of the games cups against computers. The player gets to pick which cup they want to race in, and which course they want to race as well. In the mode Time Trial the player drives around a track for a certain number of laps as quickly as they can. This mode is only available in Single Player, but is also a practice mode for the real race. VS is a single and multiplayer mode, it allows 1-4 human players to race on a single console in any track. You can versus your friends, and make it a fun and competitive game. Battle Mode is a multiplayer mode. The only item the player can get is a Bob-omg and the player gets points for hitting opponents with them. The player can have up to five Bob-ombs per character. After receiving three or four points, the player wins.

How much is Mario Kart?

Mario Kart 8 is the newest Mario Kart game, and it costs around $60-$70 depending on where you buy it from.

Where can you play Mario Kart?

You can Play Mario Kart 8 Deluxe on PlayStation, Xbox, PC, and Nintendo Switch. You can buy Mario Kart on the devices or a disk from shops like Big W, Target, JB HI FI, and other places.
